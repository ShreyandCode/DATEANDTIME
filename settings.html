Date & Time Formats

Show Formatting Placeholders

Available Placeholders:

Time:
  • H: Hour (0-23)
  • HH: Hour, 2-digit
  • h: Hour, 12-hr
  • hh: Hour, 12-hr, 2-digit
  • m: Minute
  • mm: Minute, 2-digit
  • s: Second
  • ss: Second, 2-digit
  • A: AM/PM
  • a: am/pm
Date:
  • D: Day
  • DD: Day, 2-digit
  • M: Month num
  • MM: Month num, 2-digit
  • MMM: Month, short
  • MMMM: Month, full
  • YY: Year, 2-digit
  • YYYY: Year, 4-digit
  • d: Day of week (0-6)
  • dd: Day, min
  • ddd: Day, short
  • dddd: Day, full
